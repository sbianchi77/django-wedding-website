Brunch

Who doesn't love a Sunday morning brunch

Bauer Kitchen

Located a few minutes from the Delta, Bauer Kitchen has fantastic eggs benedict and french toast. Check out their menu here.

Belmont Bistro

For those picking up their car in the morning, this spot is real close to the venue. This is a popular spot with limited seating, but definitely worth it. Here's what they have to offer.

Korner Kitchen

This one is a bit of a drive, but one of Val and Sam's favourites. It's more of a traditional bacon 'n eggs type breakfast. Check out their menu.

City Café

For those in a hurry or who prefer an on-the-go breakfast, City Café has great woodfire bagels and croissants. They have three locations around town. The Lancaster Street location is most convenient for those heading out from the Delta and the Ottawa Street location is most convenient for those at the Crowne. City Café accepts cash only.
